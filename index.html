Get To Know Me

 

Writing has been my passion for as long as I can remember. As a Technical Writing & Communications major at Carnegie Mellon University, I have learned how to use the written word to create innovative solutions to everyday problems. Now, my interests span english, technology, robotics, aerospace, business, and more!

My story starts as a competitive debater at my high school in New York. Speech & Debate was a transformative life experience for me, teaching me the importance of public speaking, "thinking off the cuff," and "getting smart quickly."

I'm always looking for opportunities in any industry which allows me to "put my heart into the work" (an Andrew Carnegie motto)! In my free time, I enjoy running, cooking, innovating, and creative writing.

My interdisciplinary experience has allowed me to pursue opportunities in various fields. I interned at the Pittsburgh Jewish Chronicle in summer 2021, Salesforce in summer 2022, IBM in summer 2023, and will be joining Ansys in fall 2023.
